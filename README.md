## The Core Methodology

### Step 1 — Data Collection

Every analysis begins with structured data intake. The client provides:

- **Date, time, and location of birth** — used to generate the Saju (Four Pillars) chart and Human Design bodygraph
- **Key life events** — specifically, the periods they found most difficult and most successful, with approximate timing
- **Current situation** — what is happening now, what area of life they want to focus on (relationships, work, self)
- **Recurring patterns** — any repeating dynamics they are already aware of

This intake is intentionally minimal. The goal is to get enough signal to begin the reverse-engineering process without overwhelming the client or introducing bias.

### Step 2 — Reverse-Engineering the Past

Before any forward-looking insight is offered, the past is mapped first.

Using the Saju chart's Daewoon (major 10-year cycles) and annual Seun (yearly cycles), a timeline is constructed of the client's energetic periods — times when their chart was in harmony with the cycles, and times when it was in friction. This timeline is then cross-referenced against the key events the client reported.

When a client says "my worst period was between 27 and 30" — and the chart shows a major friction cycle running from exactly 27 to 31 — the analysis gains a verified anchor. From that anchor, the interpretation becomes significantly more precise.

This reverse-engineering step serves two functions. First, it builds genuine trust — not through vague statements that could apply to anyone, but through specific, verifiable accuracy about things that have already happened. Second, it calibrates the entire analysis. Once the past is confirmed, the forward-looking insight is grounded in evidence rather than assumption.

### Step 3 — Structural Pattern Identification

Once the past is mapped and verified, the underlying structure is identified.

This is where the Human Design system becomes particularly powerful. Human Design categorizes people by Type (Manifestor, Generator, Manifesting Generator, Projector, or Reflector), Authority (the internal decision-making mechanism), Profile (the archetypal role one plays in life), and a detailed map of defined and undefined energy centers.

Cross-referenced with the Saju chart's analysis of innate tendencies, relationship patterns, and life themes, a highly specific picture emerges of:

- **Why this person tends to attract a particular type of partner**
- **What attachment style was formed early and why**
- **Where the decision-making process consistently breaks down**
- **Which recurring relationship dynamic is most likely to repeat**
- **What the person genuinely needs in a relationship versus what they unconsciously seek**

The distinction between what someone needs and what they repeatedly chase is often the core of the pattern. Making that visible is the central work of DecodedHeart.

### Step 4 — Insight Delivery

All analysis is delivered in written form — detailed, structured, and direct. No video calls. No scheduled sessions. The client receives a comprehensive written analysis they can return to, re-read, and sit with over time.

The writing is intentionally precise rather than poetic. Patterns are named clearly. Structures are described with specificity. The goal is not to create a feeling of wonder but to create a moment of genuine recognition — where the client reads something and thinks: *that is exactly what happens, and now I understand why.*

## What DecodedHeart Does NOT Do

Clarity about what this service is not is as important as clarity about what it is.

**DecodedHeart does not make specific date predictions.** Monthly or weekly forecasts fall below the threshold of reliable accuracy. Any service claiming to predict specific events on specific dates is operating outside what these systems can honestly deliver. DecodedHeart does not do that.

**DecodedHeart does not offer emotional validation as a service.** Clients who primarily want to be told they are right, that the other person was wrong, or that everything will be okay may find this service uncomfortable. The analysis follows the pattern wherever it leads, including into territory the client may not want to examine.

**DecodedHeart is not therapy.** The service does not diagnose, treat, or address clinical mental health conditions. It is a structured analytical and coaching service. Clients dealing with serious mental health concerns are encouraged to work with a licensed professional.

**DecodedHeart does not guarantee outcomes.** Insight is offered. What the client does with it is entirely their own responsibility.
